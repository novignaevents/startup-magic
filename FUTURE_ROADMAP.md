# NoVigna: Future Growth & Feature Roadmap

This document outlines strategic, high-impact features targeted for future development phases. These additions are designed to expand NoVigna from a matchmaking prototype into a comprehensive, sticky ecosystem for couples, vendors, and platform administrators.

---

## 1. User Portal (The Couples & Families)

### Collaborative Multi-User Planning
Weddings and large events are family affairs. This feature allows the primary user to invite "Co-Planners" with role-based access.
*   **Use Case:** Parents can view budgets and approve the catering menu, while siblings or friends can solely manage the DJ, music, and photography selections without accessing financial details.

### AI Budget Auto-Balancer
A dynamic financial safety net that actively monitors the user's spending against their stipulated budget.
*   **Use Case:** If a user splurges and goes 20% over budget on a premium Venue, the AI engine immediately calculates and suggests automated cost-saving alternatives in Decor or Catering to bring the total event cost back down to the target number.

### Guest CRM & WhatsApp RSVPs
A centralized guest list manager that removes the headache of tracking manual RSVPs.
*   **Use Case:** Users upload bulk contacts. NoVigna directly sends beautifully designed WhatsApp RSVP cards, collects seating preferences, and tracks dietary restrictions automatically in a single dashboard.

### 360° Virtual Venue Tours
Integrating immersive 3D walkthroughs of venues, halls, and mandaps directly into the venue selection page.
*   **Use Case:** Families can explore and shortlist spaces remotely, saving hours of physical site visits across the city.

---

## 2. Vendor Portal (The Partners)

### Surge & Dynamic Pricing Engine
Give vendors a toggle for "Smart Pricing" to maximize their yield during peak seasons.
*   **Use Case:** If the AI detects that a specific weekend (like a major auspicious wedding date) has overwhelming demand with few available vendors, it automatically suggests the vendor raise their base price by 15-20% for any inquiries on those dates.

### In-App E-Signatures & Auto-Contracts
Eliminate offline, messy paperwork and establish immediate legal trust.
*   **Use Case:** When an event is mutually accepted, the system instantly generates a legally binding, standardized PDF contract tailored to the event parameters that both the user and vendor sign digitally within the app.

### Vendor-to-Vendor (B2B) Marketplace
A sub-market where registered NoVigna vendors can securely trade, rent, or sub-contract with each other.
*   **Use Case:** A decorator might be short on specific premium chairs for a weekend, or a caterer might need extra serving staff. They can instantly source trusted, verified resources from other vendors on the platform.

### Milestone Escrow Payouts Tracker
Clear, transparent visibility on how and when funds are disbursed.
*   **Use Case:** Shows explicit payout stages (e.g., 20% advance, 50% midway, 30% after event completion). Funds are held securely in a platform escrow, ensuring the vendor knows the money is guaranteed while protecting the user from ghosting.

---

## 3. Admin Portal (NoVigna HQ)

### Predictive Demand Heatmaps
An AI forecasting and logistics map to actively manage supply and demand.
*   **Use Case:** The system warns the admin team: *"Bengaluru will face a 40% shortage of Makeup Artists for the November 14th weekend."* This allows the operational team to heavily push vendor onboarding/marketing in specific cities *before* the bottleneck occurs.

### Automated Quality & Fraud AI
A backend watchdog system that keeps the marketplace premium and trustworthy.
*   **Use Case:** Automatically scans new vendor portfolios for stolen or stock images. It also algorithmically flags and temporarily delists vendors whose response times crater or who receive sudden spikes in negative reviews.

### Dynamic Tiered Commissions
Moves the platform away from flat fees to an incentive-based monetization model.
*   **Use Case:** Elite vendors consistently appearing in the "Royal Roster" are rewarded with lower platform commissions (e.g., 5%), while new or lower-tier vendors pay a slightly higher rate (e.g., 12%) until they repeatedly prove their reliability.

### Unified Conflict Resolution Center
Drastically reduces support hours needed to resolve user-vendor disputes.
*   **Use Case:** If a dispute arises, admins open a single dashboard that instantly merges the user's timeline, the vendor's timeline, chat logs, and all payment milestones into one chronological screen, stripping out the "he said/she said" and allowing for instant, fact-based resolutions.
